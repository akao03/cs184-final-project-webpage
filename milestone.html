Spermulator 3D-Remake Milestone Report

Names: Sanika Bharvirkar, Circle Chen, Ashley Kao, Grant Zhao

Link to webpage: https://akao03.github.io/cs184-final-project-webpage/

We have made significant progress towards our goal as of April 20th, 2025. First, we were able to obtain a 3D sperm model and animate it using sine displacement to produce a "swimming" motion. At the same time, we were able to convert the camera view to 3D to simulate 3D movement and add glows to our 3D objects using Bloom filters. We are currently polishing our acidic patches (spheres in the backend) so they can both glow and be transparent.

The 3D model of the uterus is mostly formed and we are continually revising it before we import it to our game. We have created textures for the uterus and egg. Next steps include creating models and textures for bacteria and finalizing those of the egg alongside updating game functionality for 3D.
